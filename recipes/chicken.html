Unbelievable Chicken

The unusual combination of easy ingredients in this marinade for chicken is fabulous! Everyone who tastes it asks me to share the recipe. You will love it and the many compliments you get — I promise!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large resealable plastic bag, combine the soy sauce, vegetable oil, honey, garlic, ginger, black pepper, red pepper flakes, and green onions.
  2. Add the chicken breasts to the bag, seal it, and turn to coat the chicken evenly.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for more flavor.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the marinade.
  6. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through and no longer pink in the center.
  7. Remove from the grill and let rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  8. Serve the chicken warm, garnished with sesame seeds.
